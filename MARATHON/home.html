MARATHON MARRAKECH

The Marrakech Marathon is an annual marathon held in Marrakech,Morocco.Roughly 5000 runners turn out for the event annually.At the inaugural edition of the race in 1987 French runner Jacques Boxberger won the men's race while the women's race was won by a Moroccan 14-year-old Nadia Colombero whose win made her one of the youngest ever winners of an international level marathon
